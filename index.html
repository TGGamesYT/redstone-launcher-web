Powerful Features

Everything you need for the ultimate Minecraft experience

Dual Editions: Fancy & Fast

Choose your vibe. Fancy Edition delivers a sleek, modern UI built with Electron — perfect for aesthetics and smooth visuals. Fast Edition uses NodeGui for raw performance and minimal resource use. Your launcher, your speed.

Total Instance Freedom

Create, clone, and customize every Minecraft instance your way. Separate mods, profiles, versions, and settings — complete control with zero hassle.

Privacy First

No tracking. No analytics. No hidden uploads. Everything — from your mods to your settings — stays 100% local on your device. Your data belongs to you.

Modular Tools

From world editors to resource pack optimizers, a growing suite of tools is built right in. Simplify every part of your Minecraft experience without leaving the launcher.

Seamless Mod & Profile Management

Install, update, and organize mods or entire profiles with a single click. Spend less time setting up, more time playing.
